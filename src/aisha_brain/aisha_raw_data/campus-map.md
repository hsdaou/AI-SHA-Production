# ISC-Sharjah Campus Map

## Map Overview
The ISC-Sharjah campus is laid out on two floors. The Ground Floor is divided into Zones 1 to 5 and contains the Kindergarten wing, the gymnasium, the main administration block, the cafeteria, the labs and most lower-grade classrooms. The First Floor is Zone 6 and contains Grades 4 through 12 plus the library and multi-purpose hall. The main entrance, the atrium and reception are on the Ground Floor and are the canonical starting point for indoor navigation.

## Ground Floor — Zone Index
- Zone 1: Kindergarten wing (KG1, KG2, KG reading room, KG computer lab, KG teacher lounge, KG coordinator, KG head supervisor, KG gymnasium, KG track area)
- Zone 2: Main administrative block (atrium, main entrance, reception, principal office, vice-principal office, conference room, IT room, control room, server room, secretary, operators, front desk, coordinator offices)
- Zone 3: Cafeteria block (cafeteria seating, kitchen, dish wash, dry store, cold store, utensil wash, garbage room, bookshop, uniform shop, lost & found, staff lounge, locker rooms)
- Zone 4: Gymnasium and swimming pool block (gymnasium, swimming pool area, boys/girls changing rooms, showers, basketball court, music room G5–G8, stage, dressing rooms, old gym)
- Zone 5: Science / Senior block (biology lab, physics lab, chemistry lab, art room, video conference room, Grade 6 classrooms F–K, Grade 10 sections L/SA/SB, Grade 11 sections SA/SB/SC/SD/SE/LA, French classrooms)

## First Floor — Zone Index
- Zone 6: Upper school floor — Library, multi-purpose hall, Grades 3 to 12 classrooms, HR offices, AQC offices, university advisor office, recruitment department, social workers office, science room (G3 to G4), prayer room, gymnastic hall, swimming pool deck

## Floor-by-Floor Quick Lookup
- To find Grades KG1, KG2, 1, 2 and most of 3, go to the Ground Floor (Zones 1 to 5).
- To find Grades 4, 5, 7, 8, 9, 12 and the library, go to the First Floor (Zone 6).
- Grade 3 is split: GR3 A–D are on the Ground Floor and GR3 E–H are on the First Floor (Zone 6).
- Grade 6 is split: GR6 A–D are on the First Floor (Zone 6) and GR6 E–K are on the Ground Floor (Zone 5).
- Grade 10 is split: GR10 L, SA, SB are on the Ground Floor (Zone 5) and GR10 SC, SD, SE are on the First Floor (Zone 6).
- Grade 11 is entirely on the Ground Floor in Zone 5.
- The Main Entrance, Atrium, Cafeteria, Gymnasium, Swimming Pool, Labs and Principal Office are all on the Ground Floor.
- The Library, Multi-Purpose Hall and University Advisor Office are on the First Floor.

## Navigation Notes for AI-SHA
- AI-SHA is parked at the Charging Station on the Ground Floor when idle and uses the Atrium near the Main Entrance as the reference origin (0, 0) of the SLAM map.
- The Ground Floor is fully reachable by AI-SHA's mecanum drive. Reaching the First Floor (Zone 6) requires assistance because there is no elevator on AI-SHA's path.
- When asked for the location of a room, AI-SHA should reply with the floor (Ground Floor or First Floor), the zone (1–6) and the nearest landmark from this knowledge base, and only offer to navigate when the target is on the Ground Floor.
